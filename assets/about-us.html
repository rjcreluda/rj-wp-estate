Who are we?

Maison Goliathus, based in Southampton is a general construction company, a real estate agency, a relocation agency and a concierge. Our office, which has a dozen professionals, is there to analyse and support you in the project of your life. Through our expertise in the various phases of construction, we make every effort to develop and find architectural solutions that meet your desires, your needs and your budget while ensuring excellent value for money. Our main asset is the follow-up of your project in a professional way. Managing the latter is not a trivial process. Indeed, it is one of the most important stages in a construction.
Thanks to this service and the relationship of trust that we can build with you, we assure you a most pleasant experience throughout the duration of the work. This approach guarantees our main objectives which are: - expenses management; - respect of deadlines; - quality of construction; and thus have the best quality / price ratio in the area. Do you want to sell or buy properties, land, or a business? Our team is also at your disposal to offer you a personalised and efficient service in order to best meet your expectations. Listening, advice, competence and know-how are our watchwords in this area.
